Vision and Mission

With a main concern of our customers well-being, our target is to offer a perfect synergy between automation and light.

Our vision

Our intelligent solutions improve quality of life and energy efficiency at your home.

Our mission

Offering demand-driven automation and lighting solutions for your home and well-being.

Experience and quality

HighLight benefits from an interdisciplinary group with experience in diverse fields from electronics, programming and automation, to design and marketing. Thanks to this expertise and our exceptionally high quality standards, we are willing to offer our customers first-class products that will stand out in efficiency, safety, durability, quality and innovation.
