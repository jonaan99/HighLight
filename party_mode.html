Party mode

Make your parties shine

What is this for?

Have you ever had a party at your house but you thought that something was missing? Here is the answer.

With our product you will be able to create a real party atmosphere with the LED lights. And even more, you could make the LED lights work at the rhythm of the music in 7 different ways!

How does it work?

The working method is really simple and it can work both ways, wireless or with wires.

Wireless

The Raspberry Pi has to have the LED band connected, but also a USB soundcard plugged in into one of the USB ports. To that USB soundcard there needs to be connected a male jack-to-two-female-jack wire, one of the female-jack entrances needs to be connected to the other port of the USB soundcard via a jack-to-jack wire. The other female-jack entrance has to be connected to the speaker with a jack-to-jack wire. Finally, the phone (or any other device) has to be connected via Bluetooth or via Spotify to the Raspberry Pi, and then the magic will happen. Once the music starts on the device, the LEDs will start blinking at the rhythm of the music.

With wires

As if it was wireless, the Raspberry Pi has to have the LED band connected, and also the USB soundcard plugged in. In this case, the male jack-to-two-female-jack wire has to be used also, but in this case, the male-jack has to be connected to the smartphone (or any other device). One of the other female-jack entrances has to be connected to the speaker with a jack-to-jack wire, and the other female-jack entrance has to be connected to the USB soundcard with a jack-to-jack wire. Once everything is connected, every sound that comes from the device connected will make the party mode work.
